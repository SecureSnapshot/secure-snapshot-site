Security Baseline Review

A structured, plain-language review of foundational security controls in Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace.

Designed for small businesses that want a clearer picture of their current security posture and practical next steps.

Who this is for

Small businesses

Typically 5–200 employees looking for clarity on baseline security.

M365 or Google Workspace

Your primary email and identity platform is Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace.

No dedicated security team

You want practical, non-technical guidance without a long engagement.

What’s included

Identity and access (high level)

  • Admin accounts and privilege separation
  • MFA coverage and basic policies
  • Risky sign-in patterns (where available)

Email security basics

  • Forwarding and inbox rule checks
  • SPF/DKIM/DMARC basics (domain checks)
  • External sharing posture (high level)

Backup and recovery fundamentals

  • What’s backed up and what isn’t
  • Separation and basic restore readiness
  • Documentation you can reuse

What you receive

  • Short written summary of findings
  • Prioritised list of key risks (plain language)
  • Practical next steps to improve security
  • A walkthrough call to explain the results

Pilot program notice

This is a limited pilot to validate the review process and gather feedback.

During the pilot, reviews are offered at no cost in exchange for feedback and, if you’re happy with the outcome, a short testimonial.

This service will be offered as a paid engagement in the future. This pilot is not penetration testing and does not guarantee security.

FAQs

Is this penetration testing?

No. This is a baseline review focused on foundational controls and configuration checks.

What access do you need?

It depends on your setup. For pilot participants, we’ll discuss the minimum needed for an evidence-based review.

Will this guarantee we won’t get hacked?

No. It identifies baseline gaps and priorities. Security is ongoing and risk can’t be fully eliminated.
